🎨 UI/UX Direction
Design Concept: "Clinical Calm" — পরিষ্কার, বিশ্বাসযোগ্য, আর আধুনিক। ভয় না জাগিয়ে professional confidence দেখাবে।
Color Palette:
Primary:    #1A6BCC  (deep trust blue)
Accent:     #00C2A8  (teal-mint — dental freshness)
Background: #F8FAFB  (near white, clean)
Dark Text:  #1C2B3A  (navy-black)
Light Gray: #EDF2F7  (card backgrounds)
White:      #FFFFFF
Typography:

Display/Headings: Playfair Display (authority + warmth)
Body: Inter (clean, readable)
Labels/Badges: DM Sans (modern utility)

Signature Element: Hero section-এ একটা large tooth illustration বা smile photo-র উপর দিয়ে animated teal underline দিয়ে headline reveal হবে — "Your Healthiest Smile Starts Here"
Layout Feel: Lots of white space, soft card shadows, rounded corners (12–16px), subtle blue/teal gradients শুধু CTA buttons-এ।
